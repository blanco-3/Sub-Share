# Demo Script

## Goal

Show one clear story:

One teammate pays for Claude first. The team pays them back through the vault instead of through awkward reminders.

## Recording Flow

1. Open the home screen.
   Say: "This is for hackathon teams sharing Claude. One person pays first, everyone else commits their share upfront."

2. Create a new vault.
   Use:
   - Claude Test or Claude Pro
   - 3 teammates
   - 3 months

3. Show the review screen.
   Point out:
   - total monthly amount
   - each person’s share
   - upfront deposit requirement

4. Deploy the vault.
   Show the invite link.

5. Open the invite link as a teammate.
   Show:
   - join
   - deposit

6. Return to the creator and show the vault filling up.
   Point out that the money is already in the contract before reimbursement happens.

7. Move to the active vault.
   Show:
   - unlocked month
   - approval status
   - creator claim section

8. Use the approval path.
   Have all teammates approve one month.

9. Show the completed payback.
   Point out:
   - creator was reimbursed
   - tx hash / Basescan link

10. Close with the roadmap.
   Say:
   - "The fallback path is unanimous approval."
   - "The next step is zkTLS-based billing proof, so the creator does not depend on teammate approval after paying."

## What To Avoid

- Do not overclaim zkTLS as fully live.
- Do not spend too long on wallet setup.
- Do not switch between too many accounts unless it helps the story.
- Do not explain every smart contract detail; keep the demo user-centered.
